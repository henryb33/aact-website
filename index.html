Mission Statement

The Aeronautics & Astronautics CubeSat Team (AACT) is an organization of faculty, graduate and undergraduate students working together to design, build and test CubeSats. Our mission is to teach students the fundamentals of space systems and provide a hands-on experience that complements academic work .
